Talos IDPS

Intrusion Detection & Prevention System — PhD project

Talos IDPS is a behavioural-profiling based intrusion detection and prevention system. It is a modular system, allowing timely detection of threats and good levels of accuracy.

Modular system
Module components allow Talos IDPS to be configured to operate in a wide variety of environments and settings,
Behavioural-profiling
Applies novel behavioural profiling techniques for detecting and responding to threats, avoiding the pitfalls of signature-based and rule based systems.
Self-learning capability
Talos IDPS has the capability and capacity to learn over time, allowing it to learn attack patterns and to predict attacks accordingly.
Note: Talos IDPS is a prototype system developed as part of a PhD research project and as such is experimental. By downloading and running the system you accept this and understand that the author is not responsible for any damage or loss of data that may occur.
